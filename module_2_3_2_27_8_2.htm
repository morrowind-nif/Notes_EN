NiParticleBomb - notes

NiParticleBomb
 
Примечание.
1. найти в nif.xml файле поиском "NiParticleBomb".
2. заменить весь имеющийся блок на этот:
    <niobject name="NiParticleBomb" abstract="0" inherit="NiParticleModifier">
        A particle modifier.
        <add name="Decay" type="float">Controls the how the force will decay with distance.</add>
        <add name="Duration" type="float">Controls how long the bomb will affect particles. .</add>
        <add name="DeltaV" type="float">Controls the acceleration the bomb will apply to particles.</add>
        <add name="Start" type="float">The time at which the bomb will being to influence particles.</add>
        <add name="Decay Type" type="DecayType">Unknown.</add>
        <add name="Symmetry Type" type="SymmetryType" ver1="4.0.0.2">Unknown.</add>
        <add name="Position" type="Vector3">The position of the mass point relative to the particle system?</add>
        <add name="Direction" type="Vector3">The direction of the applied acceleration?</add>
    </niobject>
 
Примечание.
